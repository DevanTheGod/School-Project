Our Story

Rucker's was founded in the year 1975 by a farmer known as Mr. Rusty Rucker and his kin the first Rucker's location was built on a homestead in Scottsdale, AZ The Rucker's restaurant is a family-friendly restaurant with a professional waitstaff that has been passed down many generations.
